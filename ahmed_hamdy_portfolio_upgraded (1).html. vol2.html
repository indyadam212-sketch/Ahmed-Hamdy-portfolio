About

Professional Profile

Ahmed Hamdy is an Academic Coordinator and IB & Cambridge Curriculum Specialist with over 10 years of experience in international education across Egypt and Saudi Arabia. He specialises in IB, IGCSE, and A Level programmes, academic leadership, curriculum development, and student performance improvement.

At Al-Hussan International Academy, Ahmed coordinates IB and Cambridge academic programmes, manages IGCSE and A Level examination entries and results, and supports accreditation processes including CIS and Cognia. His dual role as Head of the English Department and classroom teacher keeps him directly connected to the learner experience he helps shape.

Before joining Al-Hussan, Ahmed spent seven years at NIES School in Egypt, managing English department operations, improving student academic performance, and supervising teaching and assessments.

Ahmed holds a Cambridge PDQ in Educational Leadership (2025), is an IB Certified Teacher, and is a British Council-accredited Trainer of Trainers in IELTS — credentials that reflect his expertise in curriculum, assessment, and data-driven decision making.
